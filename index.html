About University.

Amity University
A Best University of india.

Amity University Punjab (Mohali) is the eleventh university established by the not-for-profit Amity Foundation. Being set up as a multi-disciplinary university, it offers 90+ UG and PG degree programmes along with Doctoral programmes. The university is proposed to be the hub of hi-end research, with extensive collaborations with top global universities to facilitate international exposure for its students and research opportunities for faculty.The 40-acre hi-tech campus is just 10 minutes away from Chandigarh International Airport. Amity University Punjab endeavors to emerge as a global knowledge hub, attracting and nurturing the most brilliant students who share our mission to change the world, together .
